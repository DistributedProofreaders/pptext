
“Three hours!” the trainmxn continued his growling as he went on with his lantern.
“That’s the hell o’ railroading it along the edge of the Arctic. When you git
snowed in you’re _snowed in_, an’ there ain’t no two ways about it!”

Il Boebel Bratwurst si trova a Rittersbrach, vicino Norimberga. E il suo
proprietario Claus Boebel rappresenta la quarta generazione di una famiglia
di macellai così devota alle bratwurst da averci creato un impero.
